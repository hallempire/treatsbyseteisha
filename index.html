✦ Our Story

A Family Tradition, Reimagined.

What started with Mom, Seteisha, baking and selling treats to friends and family… has grown into something much more.

Baking has always been part of our story — passed down from both sides of our family, through generations of women who created with love.

What began as a mother's vision became a young daughter's mission. Sha'noi stepped in and made it her own — reimagining the idea and shaping it into something modern, intentional, and beautifully curated.

At the same time, our oldest daughter, Shadai, brought it to life online — building the website and creating the digital home for everything we envisioned.

When we couldn't find a gift that felt both premium and affordable — even something like Ferrero Rocher didn't quite capture the moment — we decided to create it ourselves. With Mother's Day approaching, everything came together quickly.

What started as one mother's passion… became a shared family effort. Today, we create treat boxes that feel special, thoughtful, and beautifully made — for every kind of person and every kind of moment.
